The Backlog Ledger

Welcome

A short setup so the office is furnished before the contract is signed.

Backlog Ledger helps a group of friends turn their Steam backlogs into a shared challenge. One person hosts the ledger, and everyone else joins through their browser.

This wizard only runs once. Nothing leaves your server — keys, names, and prices stay yours.
