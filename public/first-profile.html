First profile setup — part 2 of 2

The Backlog Ledger

Now to make your first profile

A profile is one person on the ledger — a Steam library plus a name.

The server is set up — now we'll create your own profile and pull in your Steam library. You'll be the owner of this ledger.

Other people can add their own profiles later from the dashboard.

Your name on the ledger

How you'll appear on the leaderboard and in Discord posts.

This is just your label on the ledger — doesn't have to match your Steam name.
Used to @-mention you in the weekly Discord digest once a Discord bot is configured. You can skip this and add it later.

Link your Steam account

We'll use this to read your library — nothing else.

Paste your full profile URL, your vanity name, or your 17-digit SteamID64. Any of those work.

Couldn't find the profile? Common causes: invalid profile URL or SteamID64, a private Steam profile (game details hidden), an invalid Steam API key, or Steam being temporarily unavailable.

Pull in your Steam library

One sync now — the ledger will auto-sync every Wednesday after that.

We'll fetch every game on your linked Steam account and add it to the ledger. This usually takes a few seconds, but a very large library can take a minute.

If your Steam profile or game details are private, the sync will fail. You can keep your profile public for the sync and switch it back after — the ledger only re-syncs once a week.

Import purchase history (optional)

So the ledger knows what you actually paid.

The ledger can estimate prices from the Steam store, but real purchase history is far more accurate — it knows exactly what you paid (including sales) and which games were gifts or freebies.

You can do this now in a dedicated import page, or skip it and import later from your profile tools.

Tip: you can always reach the importer from your profile menu later.

You're in

The ledger is set up and your profile is ready.

Everything's saved. From here you can browse your library, see the leaderboard, and (when you're ready) invite friends to add their own profiles.

Need to import a purchase history later? Open your profile menu and look for Import ledger records.
